    // How It Works

    From Chat to Launch
    in 4 Simple Steps

    01

    Discovery Call

    We chat about your business, your vibe, what you love (and hate) about other websites. I'll get a feel for your brand, your audience, and what you need. Free, no pressure, just a conversation.

    02

    Design & Build

    I design your site from scratch — no templates, no cookie-cutters. You get a first draft to review, then we refine together. I'll research your competitors, pull in your reviews, build your menu, the works.

    03

    Review & Launch

    Once you're happy with every pixel, I handle the technical launch — domain, hosting, SSL, speed optimisation, SEO. Your site goes live and I stick around for 30–90 days of support depending on your plan.

    04

    Grow & Maintain

    If you're on a maintenance plan, I handle all the ongoing stuff — menu changes, new reviews, seasonal updates, security patches. You focus on running your business. I keep your site running beautifully.
