About Macready's

A Culinary Destination

Macready's Restaurant is a premier fine dining establishment located in the heart of Sherborne, offering an exceptional culinary experience. With over 45 satisfied Google reviews and a 4.5-star rating, we pride ourselves on delivering outstanding food, impeccable service, and an elegant atmosphere.

Our chef-led kitchen specializes in fresh seafood and seasonal ingredients, creating dishes that celebrate British cuisine with contemporary flair.
